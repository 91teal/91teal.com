About

A considered retreat in the heart of the Pines

91 Teal is a fully renovated, design-led beach house located one block from the Fire Island Pines harbor and ferry dock. Gut renovated in 2023 by BoND, the home reflects a modern architectural sensibility paired with an atmosphere of calm. It is positioned in one of the most desirable locations, only a short walk from everything.

Inside, the layout is open and airy with warm natural light and thoughtful details throughout. The home is owner occupied when not rented, which means materials are intentional, furnishings are elevated, and every part of the experience feels carefully maintained.

91 Teal sleeps eight guests across four bedrooms. Each room has its own mini split climate control system. Three king beds and one queen bed offer generous comfort, and the home includes three full baths, one half bath, and an outdoor shower.
